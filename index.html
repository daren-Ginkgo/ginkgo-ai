What it does

Your first draft, done for you

Upload the case material and the engine produces a tidy first draft in our approved wording and layout. You stay in control — every document is yours to review, edit and sign off.

📄

Suitability reports

Drafts a full suitability report in the Ginkgo house style — summary first, client-friendly headings, and clear cost, plan and income tables.

🗓️

Annual advice meeting docs

Prepares your AAM documents for pre- and post-meeting, following our checklists so nothing important gets missed.

✅

Built-in compliance check

Runs a compliance check, flags genuine gaps as "information required", and never invents missing client facts.

📝

Ready-to-use Word files

Outputs polished Word documents in our font and layout — a clean client report plus a separate adviser QA file.

How it works

Getting started takes a minute

1

Add your material

Upload the case documents (PDF, Word, text or CSV) or paste them in. Use dummy or anonymised data only.

2

Check the data

The engine reviews what you've added and lists what's there and what's missing. Fill any gaps, then re-check.

3

Build, review and sign off

Generate the Word documents, read them through carefully, make any changes, and apply your sign-off.

Before you start

A few important ground rules

  • This is a prototype. It's here to test the experience, not the finished secure product.
  • Dummy or anonymised data only. Please don't put real client information into the app.
  • Every draft needs human sign-off. The engine produces a first draft — you remain responsible for checking and approving it.
  • It won't invent facts. Genuine gaps are flagged as "information required" rather than filled in.
