What it does

Your firm's first draft, done for you

Upload the case material and the engine produces a tidy first draft in your firm's approved wording and layout. Your advisers stay in control — every document is theirs to review, edit and sign off.

📄

Suitability reports

A full suitability report in your house style — summary first, client-friendly headings, and clear cost, plan and income tables. Covers investment, pension switch, drawdown and new business cases.

🗓️

Annual advice meetings

Pre- and post-meeting AAM documents that follow your checklists, plus a current-versus-proposed CRM update for a human to approve.

🤝

First client meetings

Prospect introduction documents: a pre-meeting briefing and agenda, a follow-up email and introduction report, an initial-disclosure check and a first fact find. A first meeting is not advice, and the engine keeps it that way.

🛡️

IHT & Whole of Life

A complete pension-funded Whole of Life protection pack: demands and needs, the plan explained, pension funding and sustainability, and a covering email — with the inheritance tax maths done properly.

🧮

WOL Ready-Reckoner

An instant, indicative Whole of Life cost by age, basis and sum assured, for review meetings and first conversations before any full advice. Indicative only, never a quote.

✅

Built-in compliance check

Every document runs a compliance or quality check, flags genuine gaps as "information required", and never invents missing client facts. You get a clean client report plus a separate adviser QA file in Word.

Good to know

How we keep it safe and honest

  • Demo stage. What you can try today is a working demonstration of the experience, not the finished secure, multi-tenant product.
  • Dummy or anonymised data only. Real client information stays out of the demo until a firm's secure setup is signed off.
  • Every draft needs human sign-off. The engine produces a first draft — your adviser remains responsible for checking and approving it.
  • It won't invent facts. Genuine gaps are flagged as "information required" rather than filled in. The tool never marks anything "compliant"; it reports a check result for a human.
